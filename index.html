Cafe History

Coffee originated in Ethiopia and spread to the Arabian Peninsula in the 15th century. It gained popularity in Europe, leading to the emergence of coffeehouses. Cultivation expanded to regions like Java and Brazil. Today, coffee is a globally consumed beverage with diverse flavors and brewing methods.

Espresso

Ristretto

The most famous coffee beverage is the espresso,

which is a strong coffee drink prepared by passing hot water under high pressure through ground coffee beans. Espresso is known for its thick consistency, rich flavor, and aromatic aroma. It is also used as a base for preparing a variety of other coffee beverages such as cappuccino, latte, mocha, and others
